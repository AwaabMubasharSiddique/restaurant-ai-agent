THE OLIVE BRANCH — RESTAURANT INFORMATION & MENU

ABOUT US
The Olive Branch is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant serving fresh,
seasonal dishes inspired by the coasts of Greece, Italy, and Lebanon. We have
been part of the neighborhood since 2014.

HOURS
Open daily: 11:00 AM – 10:00 PM
Kitchen stops taking orders 30 minutes before closing (last orders 9:30 PM).
Happy hour: Monday – Friday, 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM.

LOCATION & CONTACT
Address: 218 Cedar Street, Riverside, Springfield, 62704
Phone: (555) 014-2287
Email: hello@theolivebranch.example
We are on the corner of Cedar Street and 3rd Avenue, two blocks from Riverside Park.

PARKING & ACCESS
Free street parking after 6:00 PM. A paid public lot is located behind the building
on 3rd Avenue. The restaurant is fully wheelchair accessible, including restrooms.

RESERVATION POLICY
Reservations are recommended for parties of all sizes, especially on weekends.
We hold reserved tables for 15 minutes past the reservation time.
Large parties of 8 or more should call ahead; a set menu may apply.
All reservation requests are reviewed and confirmed by our front-of-house team.

CANCELLATION POLICY
Please give at least 2 hours notice for cancellations. Parties of 8+ that cancel
with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a $15 per-guest fee.

DIETARY & ALLERGENS
We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options, marked on the menu.
Please tell your server about any allergies — many dishes can be adapted.
Our kitchen handles nuts, dairy, gluten, and shellfish; we cannot guarantee a
fully allergen-free environment.

DRESS CODE
Smart casual. No specific dress code is enforced.

CHILDREN & GROUPS
Families are welcome; a children's menu and high chairs are available.
We host private events and can accommodate groups up to 40 in our back room.

PAYMENT
We accept all major credit cards, debit, and contactless payment. Cash accepted.
An 18% gratuity is added automatically for parties of 6 or more.
